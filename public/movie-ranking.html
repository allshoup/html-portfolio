The Top Three Movies of All Time

1. The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers

A masterclass in the middle-child of trilogies. The introduction to the Ents and the dialogue of the Urukai is unmatched.


2. The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

"Mountains, Gandelf, mountains!"


3. The Lord of the Rings: Return of the King

Aragorn's speech at the Black Gate. Enough said.
